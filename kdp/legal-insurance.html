Legal Insurance

Protecting you and your family from legal complications is important. ARAG can help with your legal needs. Legal Insurance covers the most frequently needed personal legal matters. Services include general telephone advice and office consultations, living wills, real estate matters, powers of attorney, document review, debt matters, and family law (adoption, guardianship, and prenuptial agreements).

COVERED LEGAL ISSUES INCLUDES:

  • Consumer and Fraud Protection Issues
  • Debt-Related Matters
  • Wills and Estate Planning
  • Dispute with a Landlord
  • Real Estate Matters
  • Government Benefits
  • Family Law
  • Small Claims Court
  • Civil Damage Claims (Defense)
  • Tax Issues
  • Traffic Matters, and more!

PLAN FEATURES:

  • You can elect to cover your spouse and children.
  • The cost is conveniently deducted from your paycheck.
  • Coverage is individually owned, which means you can take your coverage with you if you change jobs or retire.
