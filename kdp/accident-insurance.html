Accident Insurance

Even when you live well, accidents happen. Treatment can be vital to recovery, but it can also be expensive. If an accident keeps you away from work during recovery, the financial worries can grow quickly. Most major medical insurance plans only pay a portion of bills. Accident Insurance is designed to help cover the out-of-pocket medical expenses and extra bills you may incur as a result of an accident. The plan pays benefits directly to you – not a doctor or hospital – for injuries and accident-related expenses. You can use the money however you choose.

COVERED INJURIES AND ACCIDENT-RELATED
EXPENSES INCLUDE:

  • Fractures
  • Disclocations
  • Hospitalizations
  • Physical Therapy
  • Emergency Room Treatment
  • Transportation
  • Lodging, and more!

PLAN FEATURES:

  • You receive benefits for injuries that you experience on or off-the-job, and for other factors like emergency room care, anesthesia, follow-up treatment, and more.
  • You can elect to cover your spouse and children.
  • The cost is conveniently deducted from your paycheck.
  • Coverage is individually owned, which means you can take your coverage with you if you change jobs or retire.
  • You’re guaranteed coverage without having to answer health questions.
  • Every year, each covered family member can receive $50 for getting a routine health screening test.
  • Rates are shown during the enrollment process.
