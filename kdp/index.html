How To Enroll

Three Things to know before you enroll

02

Who Is Eligible For Benefits?

All non-union and union employees who are not offered a multi-employer health care plan are eligible for voluntary benefits as long as they are regularly scheduled to work 30 or more hours per week.

What Happens If I Don't Enroll?

Annual Enrollment is your one opportunity throughout the year to enroll in benefits. If you do not enroll in these benefits, you will not be able to enroll until Annual Enrollment in 2018.

When Can I Change My Coverage

You will not have another opportunity to elect coverage until the next Annual Enrollment unless you have a qualifying life event before then.
