Interested In Our Custom Solutions?

Contact us to learn more about custom communication solutions to engage your workforce. Aon’s VBES Communications Team will lead a discovery session to discuss your options and develop a strategic campaign that best meets your needs.

Custom campaigns can include language translations, color matching and image choices, enrollment toolkits and more. Along with digital/mobile solutions such as social media integration, HTML email blasts, and bulk SMS mass texting.

Please note that, in order to provide exceptional service, 20 weeks lead time will be required to complete custom campaigns.
