Telemedicine


For team members and covered dependents enrolled in the Group Health Plan, finding time to visit a doctor on short notice is much easier with access to a convenient telemedicine program. This benefit offers a quick and easy way to get in touch with board certified doctors, 24/7!

Instead of high cost emergency room care, you can have a video visit with a doctor from your computer, tablet, or smartphone. The doctors you speak with can treat certain medical issues, and can even write prescriptions if needed.


Examples of addressed conditions include:

  • Cold & Flu
  • Sore Throat
  • Pink Eye
  • Urinary Tract Infection
  • Skin Issues & Rashes
  • Diarrhea & Vomiting
  • Sports Injuries
  • Psychology

For medical consultations, the service has a $20 copay for those enrolled in the Traditional PPO. Those who are enrolled in the Health Investment Plan will pay the full negotiated rate of the consultation until the plan deductible is met. Coinsurance will apply after deductible is met and covered participants will be responsible for 20% of Plan Allowance.

Download the Doctor on Demand app from the App Store or Google Play. You can also access care on the web via doctorondemand.com/tyson.
