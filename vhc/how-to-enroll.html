Now is the time to consider whether your current benefits package still meets your family’s health and financial needs now and in the future. All benefits you elect during Open Enrollment become effective April 1, 2019.

Benefits Open Enrollment takes place February 8 through 28.

VHC is making some changes to the benefits plan for 2019. As a result, all employees are encouraged to speak with a Benefits Counselor during this year’s Open Enrollment.

Two Ways To Enroll:

NEW this year, you will have two options for completing enrollment. All benefit eligible employees will have access to a one-on-one session with a professional Benefits Counselor. Your Benefits Counselor can explain your benefit options, answer your questions, and help you make the benefit choices that are right for you and your family.

  • Onsite: Counselors will be available onsite at the Benefits Enrollment Café to provide enrollment assistance and help you make informed decisions about your benefits plan (see schedule below). Take advantage of this opportunity to get the help you need. Visit https://benefitsgo.qc.univers.aon.net/virginiahospitalcenter to schedule your appointment with a Benefits Counselor.
