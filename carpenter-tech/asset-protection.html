Permanent life insurance completes your life insurance protection by providing a benefit to your loved ones. The benefit can be used to cover funeral costs and any other final expenses, which might include shared credit card debt or unpaid medical bills.

As long as the cost-effective premium payments are maintained, the policy never expires, and the premium always remains the same.

Permanet Life Plan Features
Family Coverage - You can purchase coverage for yourself, your spouse, your children, and/or your grandchildren. Portable Coverage - This policy is portable, which means you can take it with you even if you leave Galaxy Corporation (though the premium will no longer be paid through payroll deduction).
Guaranteed Issue - Acceptance is guaranteed. No medical exams are required if you choose this coverage when initially offered.

Why Is This Recommended?

Aside from funeral costs that average more than $7,000, you may also have credit card debt shared with a loved one or other final expenses. These are costs that will burden your loved ones, siphoning from savings or from term life insurance, which is intended for other purposes.
