Pet Insurance

PacifiCorp offers Pet Insurance through Nationwide to help offset the cost of caring for your pet. The plan covers a variety of medical treatments from preventive care to accidents and illnesses. Pet Insurance is conveniently paid through your monthly payroll deductions. If you miss the Open Enrollment deadline to enroll with a Benefits Counselor, you will be able to enroll any time throughout the year with Nationwide by calling 877-738-7874

If you would like to find out more information about Pet Insurance after Open Enrollment, please call:

  • customer service number for questions if you are already enrolled in Pet at 800-540-2016
  • customer service number for questions if you are not enrolled with Pet at 877-738-7874
  • to enroll in Pet after Open Enrollment at 877-738-7874
  • to cancel at 800-540-2016.

PLAN FEATURES:

  • Use any vet and get back 90% reimbursement on the bill.
  • No age limits or age-based premium increases.
  • More than just accident and illness coverage.
  • Exclusive for PacifiCorp employees.
