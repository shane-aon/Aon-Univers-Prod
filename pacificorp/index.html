Open Enrollment is Here!

October 22 - November 9, 2018



This Voluntary Benefits Portal is where your Voluntary Benefits information is organized in one easy to use place. It's where you'll learn about all of your Voluntary Benefit options available to you and where you can enroll when you are hired or enroll annually during the Open Enrollment period.

No one expects to suffer from a serious illness or accident, but the financial consequences can be a real challenge.

Treating a major illness or injury normally means that out-of-pocket expenses can be quite high, especially with a high deductible health plan. Critical Illness, Accident and Hospital Indemnity Insurance helps cover your deductible and coinsurance until you reach the out-of-pocket maximum. Whole Life Insurance is an individual insurance policy designed to provide a death benefit to you beneficiaries if something should happen to you.

You may only enroll in these Voluntary Benefits during your 2019 Open Enrollment period.

PacifiCorp also offers Pet Insurance to help offset the cost of caring for your pet. The plan covers a variety of medical treatments from preventive care to accident and illnesses. PacifiCorp employees are able to enroll in Pet Insurance by calling Nationwide directly at 877-738-7874.

PacifiCorp offers several Voluntary Benefits to help you protect your income, family, pets and plan for your future and the future of your family.
