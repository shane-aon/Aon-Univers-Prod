HBC WELLNESS INCENTIVE

Posted on March 2019

WHAT IS HBC’s WELLNESS INCENTIVE?


To positively impact Associate’s health and to minimize both your and HBC’s overall healthcare costs through wellness, HBC’s Wellness Incentive program focuses on getting you:


  • Engaged with your personal physician through an Annual Wellness Visit, and

  • Aware of ways to enhance your personal health and well-being through the completion of your confidential Health Risk Assessment (HRA).

WELLNESS INCENTIVE CONTRIBUTIONS


If you are enrolled in Associate Only medical coverage through HBC and you complete the wellness requirements by March 31, you are eligible to receive a $250* contribution into your HSA.

If you are enrolled for associate plus spouse/domestic partner coverage, or for associate plus family coverage, if your spouse/domestic partner completes the wellness requirements by March 31, you will receive an additional $250* contribution to your HSA – for a total HBC contribution of $500. If only you or only your spouse/domestic partner complete the requirements, you will receive the $250 HSA contribution.

If you are enrolled in Associate + Child(ren) coverage, only you have to complete the wellness requirements to earn up to the full $500* employer contribution into your HSA.

Wellness Incentive contributions are in addition to the automatic employer contributions you receive ($250 single for Single coverage or $500 for Associate with Child(ren), Spouse/Domestic Partner or Family coverage for enrolling in the Gold or Silver HBC medical plan and opening an HSA.

*subject to reduction according to the proration schedule below.

HOW DO I MEET THE WELLNESS INCENTIVE REQUIRMENTS?


To be eligible for the Wellness Incentive, you must complete the following requirements:


  • An annual Physical Exam* (obtained after July 1, 2018

    • AND


  • A confidential, online Cigna Health Risk Assessment (completed after January 1, 2019)

An annual well woman visit with an OB/GYN is considered your Annual Physical Exam for purposes of meeting this requirement.

The online Health Assessment is a brief, confidential questionnaire that evaluates your current health and indicates if you are at risk for certain conditions. It also provides recommendations you may take toward better health. Access your Health Assessment at www.myCigna.com.

Both requirements must be completed for you to receive your 2019 Wellness Incentive. Your Incentive amount will be based on the date you (or your spouse/domestic partner, if applicable) complete your second of the two requirements. To receive the full Incentive you must complete both requirements by March 31, 2019.

If you (or your spouse/domestic partner) complete both requirements after March 31, 2019, your Wellness Incentive contribution will be reduced as follows:

Follow these steps to complete your Health Risk Assessment:


  • Register and/or log onto your myCigna account at www.myCigna.com.
  • In the top left corner, click on the My Health tab.
  • Click on "Take My Health Assessment."
  • Complete the Health Assessment and view your results.

Cigna will automatically report your completion of the Health Risk Assessment to HBC for purposes of the Wellness Incentive, but will never share your health information with HBC or any other party without your written authorization.
