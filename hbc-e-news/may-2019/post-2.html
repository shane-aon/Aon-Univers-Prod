HBC Associate and Family Assistance (Paid in Full)

Posted on May 2019

Assistance for you and your family members paid in full by HBC

HBC understands that the stresses of life, family and work can sometimes be overwhelming. We all need some “assistance” from time to time. That is why HBC provides Optum’s “Live and Work Well” Employee Assistance Program to all Associates, full and part-time, and to your immediate family and household members, AT NO COST TO YOU.

“Live and Work Well” is a comprehensive suite of services and information to help you find the support you need to live your best life.

Access Live and Work Well at the website https://www.liveandworkwell.com, set up your HealthSafe ID on the registration page, and sign in. Or you can enter anonymously using access code “HBC.” And, of course, you can call 866-248-4094 for confidential telephone help at any time.


Services and Information Included with Live and Work Well include:


Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Your Employee Assistance Program (EAP) is here to help you, and your family, get the most out of life. We're ready to help with the small questions and the big problems, and everything in-between, such as:


  • Addressing depression, anxiety or substance use issues
  • Stress and emotional well-being
  • Work performance
  • Getting past emotional issues or grief
  • WorkLife Services
  • Finding a provider
  • Authorization to see an EAP professional
  • Improving relationships at home or work
  • Getting the most out of your career
  • Finding child development, childcare or elder care resources

Services are completely confidential, and EAP Specialists are available by telephone 24/7. They can provide you immediate help or direct you to one of our network for providers for a no-cost, face-to-face consultation. Telephone consultations are UNLIMITED. Up to 5 no-cost, face-to-face consultations are available per issue per year. Where appropriate and available, you will be referred to behavioral health counselors that participate both in Live and Work Well’s network and the Cigna Behavioral Health network.

Call 866-248-4094 anytime for confidential help. You can request no-cost, face-to-face consultation on the Live and Work Well website or by calling 866-248-4094.

Financial & Legal Services

Legal matters are tricky and managing finances can be overwhelming. Access to financial counseling services, legal advice, including help drafting a will or assistance with a complicated legal matter should not be out of anyone’s reach. You don’t need to figure it out all alone — get free confidential access to experts.


Financial consultation: One no-cost 30-60 minute financial consultation per issue per year


Access to credentialed financial professionals for issues such as:

  • Budget management
  • College funding
  • Debt reduction
  • 401K, IRA, HSA questions
  • Estate planning
  • Bankruptcy
  • Investment plans
  • Retirement planning
  • Taxes
  • Wage garnishment

Legal consultation One no-cost 30 minute telephonic or face-to-face consultation per issue per year, and ongoing representation by an attorney at a 25% discounted rate. Help is available for a variety of issues including:


  • Consumer issues

  • Document preparation
  • Immigration
  • IRS matters
  • Living wills
  • Power of attorney
  • Probate
  • Real estate services

  • Traffic matters
  • Trusts
