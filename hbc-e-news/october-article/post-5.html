Delicious, Super-Healthy Chicken & Quinoa Chili

Posted on October 2018

Prep time: 10 minutes
Cook time: 4 hours in a Slow Cooker / Crockpot
Serves: 8
Calories: About 276 per serving


Ingredients:

  • 3/4 pound (or a little more) uncooked boneless skinless chicken breasts
  • 3/4 cup uncooked quinoa
  • 1 can (28 ounces) can crushed tomatoes
    • <
  • 1 can (7 ounces) can diced green chilies
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup yellow onion diced
  • 2 peppers, chopped (use green, red or yellow combination for flavor & appearance)
  • 2 (15 ounces EACH) cans kidney beans
  • 1 (15 ounces) can corn
  • 1 (15 ounces) can black beans
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 packet chili seasoning (your choice - mild, medium or hot) cayenne pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro diced jalapeno to taste (optional)
  • Garnishes and add-ins: fresh lime, green onion, cheddar cheese, sour cream, your favorite pepper sauce

Instructions:

  1. Lightly grease your 6 quart slow cooker with nonstick spray.
  2. Cut the chicken breasts in half
  3. Rinse the quinoa in a fine mesh sieve. (This removes the bitter saponin)
  4. Add the chicken breasts, quinoa, un-drained crushed tomatoes, diced green chilies, garlic, onion, and chopped peppers to the slow cooker.
  5. Drain and rinse the kidney beans, corn, and black beans. Add to the slow cooker with the broth and packet of chili seasoning. Add cayenne pepper if desired.
  6. Cover the slow cooker and set to high. Cook for 3-5 hours or until the quinoa has "popped" and the chicken is completely cooked through and easily shredded.
  7. Shred the chicken, stir everything, and add cilantro.
  8. Taste and add seasonings to desired preference.
  9. Serve with a fresh squeeze of lime, green onions if desired, a scoop of sour cream, and some freshly shredded cheddar cheese.
  10. Enjoy immediately. Try it with freshly baked corn bread.
