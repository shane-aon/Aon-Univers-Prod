Beef Pad Thai Recipe

Posted on April 2019

Delicious and healthy beef pad thai bulked up with lots of healthy veggies like matchstick carrots, snap peas and scallions. Using whole-grain brown-rice pad thai noodles adds an additional 3 grams fiber to each serving. Ingredients

Ingredients:


  • 8 ounces pad thai brown rice noodles
  • 2 teaspoons sunflower oil
  • ¾ pound lean ground beef
  • 2 cups stringless snap peas
  • 2 cups matchstick carrots
  • ½ cup chopped scallions
  • ½ cup pad thai sauce
  • Chopped peanuts and lime wedges for serving

Preparation


Prep Time: 20 minutes

Cooking Time: 20 minutes


  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook noodles in boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1 cup cooking liquid and drain the noodles.

  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium-high heat. Add beef, and cook, crumbling with a wooden spoon, until browned, 4 to 5 minutes. Add snap peas, carrots and scallions, and cook, stirring often, until the peas are bright green, 2 to 3 minutes.

  3. Add sauce, the noodles and about half of the reserved cooking liquid; stir with tongs to coat. Reduce heat to medium-low; simmer, adding more of the reserved cooking liquid to moisten if necessary and stirring occasionally, until the vegetables are crisp-tender, 2 to 3 minutes longer.

Serve hot with chopped peanuts and lime wedges.


Nutrition information

Serving size: 1¾ cups


Per serving:
465 calories 33 mcg folate 23 mg vitamin C
13 g fat (4 g saturated fat) 55 mg cholesterol 45 mg calcium
9 g fiber 14 g sugars 4 mg iron
65 g carbohydrates 10 g added sugars 894 mg sodium
23 g protein 6,493 IU vitamin A 486 mg potassium
