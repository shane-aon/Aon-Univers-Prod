Featured Vendor - Color Genomics

Posted on April 2019

In support of our commitment to families and our Associate’s physical, mental and financial health, HBC offers complimentary genetic testing through Color Genomics to all Associates enrolled in an HBC medical plan. There is no cost to Associates for this confidential service.

The following article has been provided by Color Genomics.


How

The genetic testing is done by a simple saliva test. Once you provide your sample in the kit, you simply mail it back and results will be provided in approximately 3-4 weeks. You will then have the opportunity to discuss your results and any suggested next steps with a genetic counselor. If you do carry a mutation, your direct blood relatives are eligible to participate in testing for only $50.


What

The Color service includes the following results:


Hereditary Cancer

Analyzes 30 genes, including BRCA1 and BRCA2, in which a mutation can increase one’s likelihood of developing hereditary breast, ovarian, uterine, colorectal, melanoma, pancreatic, stomach, or prostate cancers.


Heart Health

Analyzes 30 genes commonly associated with hereditary heart conditions that can have actionable treatment plans, including cardiomyopathies, arrhythmias, arteriopathies, and hereditary high cholesterol.


Medication Management

Helps you and your provider understand which medications are best for you based on your genes in order to minimize harmful side effects and get more effective care.


Color Discovery

Get insights from your DNA on an ongoing basis to help you learn more about common traits that are influenced by your genes, such as lactose intolerance and alcohol flush.
