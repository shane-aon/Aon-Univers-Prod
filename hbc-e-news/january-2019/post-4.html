NEW MEDICAL PLAN PROGRAMS FOR 2019

Posted on Janaury 2019

Read below for information on HBC’s new medical programs to help you manage your health and healthcare expenses in 2019.


ConsumerMedical – YOUR MEDICAL ALLY!


ConsumerMedical (CM) provides independent, confidential, one-on-one support to help you and your family make informed decisions about medical care and treatment at every point in your healthcare journey. CM’s services to participants in HBC’s medical plans include:


  • Surgical Decision Support is REQUIRED for four non-emergency surgeries (lower back surgery, hip or knee replacement, weight loss surgery or hysterectomy) to avoid penalties and receive an incentive. Click here for more information.

  • Expert medical guidance for any condition, no matter how complex or rare.

  • CM’s claims advocates are neutral experts who offer independent guidance to help you understand and resolve problems and reduce the stress of dealing with insurance and billing issues.

Click Here for more information on ConsumerMedical

Cigna Health Matters Complete


HBC is adding even more resources and capabilities from Cigna to provide HBC medical plan participants targeted support throughout complex and specialty treatments. Associates with significant medical needs and high medical risk will be offered increased Cigna Medical Director support, Social Worker support, pre-admission and post-admission support and specialized discharge planning through your One Guide team. As always, these services are completely confidential and provided only with your consent

Maven Maternity Program


Maven Maternity is a comprehensive, digital, 15-month program that provides unlimited access to Maven’s virtual practitioner network, a dedicated Care Concierge, and customizable resources/ curriculum in one app. HBC medical plan participants are eligible to enroll in Maven Maternity at no cost).

Click Here to see more information on Maven.

Omada


Omada Health is a digital behavioral medicine company that inspires and enables people to change the habits that put them most at risk for chronic conditions like heart disease and type 2 diabetes.

Omada Health combines proven behavioral science, the power of professional health coaches and peer groups, connected technology, and world-class design to provide at-risk individuals with the knowledge, tools and social support proven to improve health.

Click Here to see more information on Omada.

Color


Color Genomics (Color) provides genetic testing services to help you understand your genetic risk for common hereditary cancers, inherited heart conditions and medication management. All U.S.-based full-time associates 18 years old or older are eligible to access Color’s personalized genetic testing and counseling services at no cost (HBC pays the full cost of this program).

Click Here to see more information on Color.
