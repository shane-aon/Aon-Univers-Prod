END OF YEAR FLEXIBLE SPENDING ACCOUNT TIPS

Posted on Janaury 2019

So, you have reached the end of the calendar year, and you still have unused Flexible Spending Account funds available for use. What do you do now?

First, check your account balance with ConnectYourCare, HBC’s FSA administrator. You can do this by logging in at http://www.connectyourcare.com. You will also receive a letter from ConnectYourCare with information on your 2018 FSA balance.

Second, use remaining funds for eligible expenses during the Grace Period. If you did not use all your FSA funds in 2018, you have until March 15, 2019 to incur expenses eligible for FSA reimbursement.

Next, submit all 2018 eligible expenses to ConnectYourCare for reimbursement no later than June 15, 2019. That includes all 2018 expenses and those incurred by March 15, 2019. If you don’t submit by then, you lose your funds!

Remember that you can use your Limited Purpose FSA for eligible vision and dental expenses for you, your spouse and your eligible dependents.

If you were enrolled in the General Purpose FSA you can use your unused funds for:

  • Acupuncture treatments
  • Allergy medication (over-the-counter and prescription)
  • Aspirin and other over-the-counter pain relievers
  • Blood pressure monitors
  • Breast pumps
  • Breastfeeding classes
  • Childbirth classes
  • Chiropractic care
  • Condoms and other contraceptives
  • Contact lenses
  • Cough drops and syrup
  • Diabetic monitors, test kits, strips and supplies
  • Eye exams
  • Eyeglasses
  • Fertility monitors
  • Fertility treatments
  • First aid kits
  • Flu shots
  • Hearing aids and batteries
  • Infertility treatments
  • Insulin
  • Lasik
  • Mileage for travel to and from health care appointments
  • Night guards
  • Physical therapy
  • Pregnancy tests
  • Prescription drugs
  • Prescription sunglasses
  • Over-the-counter reading glasses
  • Smoking cessation programs
  • Sunscreen (with SPF 15 or higher)
  • Therapy
  • Walking aids like canes, walkers and crutches

You can also use your 2018 General Purpose FSA funds to pay for copays, coinsurance payments and deductibles – from January 1, 2018 through March 15, 2019.

Remember, the deadline for submitting claims is 6/15/2019 for active Associates, or 90 days following termination date for terminated Associates. Any remaining funds will be forfeited. Also, after March 15, 2019, your payment card may not be used to pay for 2018 plan year expenses. You can submit reimbursement requests through June 15, 2019 using the CYC mobile app or online at www.connectyourcare.com.
