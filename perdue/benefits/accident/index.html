Compass Accident Insurance

AVAILABLE ONLY DURING THE ANNUAL FALL ENROLLMENT EACH YEAR

Compass Accident Insurance offered by Voya Employee Benefits is issued and underwritten by ReliaStar Life Insurance Company, a member of the Voya® family of companies. Voluntary accident insurance provides benefits for covered specific injuries and events resulting from a covered accident while off the job. You may purchase Accident Insurance for yourself, your spouse and your children.

Accident Insurance helps to cover the out-of-pocket expenses that result from an accident injury. Accident Insurance is a limited benefit policy. It is not health insurance and does not satisfy the requirement of minimum essential coverage under the Affordable Care Act.

The following is a partial summary of some of the accident benefits that are available

  • Hospital confinement
  • Medical equipment
  • Lodging
  • Transportation
  • Physical therapy

Plan Features

  • Associates can enroll their spouse and/or children in the plan as long as the employee enrolls in coverage.
  • There are no health questions or physical exams required.
  • Coverage is portable — you can take your coverage with you if you change jobs or retire.
  • Premiums are paid through convenient payroll deductions

You will receive detailed information on the plan and costs during your enrollment

This is a summary of benefits only. A complete description of benefits, limitations, exclusions and termination of coverage will be provided in the certificate of insurance and riders. All coverage is subject to the terms and conditions of the group policy. If there is any discrepancy between this document and the group policy documents, the policy documents will govern. To keep coverage in force, premiums are payable up to the date of coverage termination.
