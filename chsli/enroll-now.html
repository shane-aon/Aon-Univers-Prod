Benefits Enrollment

To find out if you are eligible to enroll in benefits, visit the Enrollment Eligibility page.

  • Visit the Online Enrollment Portal
    Visit the Benefits Portal below to complete the step-by-step enrollment process. The Enrollment Portal is available 24/7 via computer or mobile device, so you can enroll when it’s convenient for you.


  • Call the Enrollment Center
    You may call the Enrollment Center at 1-855-874-0448, Monday through Friday 9 am - 6 pm EST (excluding holidays).

    • After You Enroll, Review Your Confirmation Statement


    After completing your enrollment session, you can log in to the Benefits Portal to review and print your confirmation statement. Please review it carefully to be sure your elections are correct and make any necessary changes before the enrollment period ends.






Dependent Documentation

If you have a dependent enrolled in the healthcare plan, you will be asked to provide documentation to prove eligibility. Documents will be verified by Consova.

A partial list of documents you may be asked to provide includes:


  • Marriage Certificate
  • Federal Tax Return
  • Birth Certificate
  • Adoption Certificate
  • Court Order/QMCSO

Please be assured and understand that any/all information you provide to Consova is kept secure and confidential, at ALL times.

If you have any questions about this process or need assistance, please call Consova’s Dependent Eligibility Verification Assistance Center at
1-844-872-1592 Monday through Friday, 10 am–6 pm Eastern Time.
