Questions & Contacts


What resources are there to help me make benefit enrollment decisions?

This website is your best resource to find information that will assist you in making your enrollment decisions. It provides plan design details, rates, educational videos, comparison tools, Legal Notices and much more. It is accessible from any computer or mobile device to allow family and other dependents to review this information. You can also call Anthem at 1-844-855-1948 and/or Cigna at 1-800-244-6224 for specific coverage and design questions about the medical and dental plans. You will also have access to a benefits counselor during benefits enrollment, who is educated on our plan offerings, and will be able to assist you in your decision-making.

If I have questions about my medical plan, where can I get answers?

Anthem is able to answer your personal medical coverage questions for the plans. You may call 1-844-855-1948.

I am a newly hired BMC associate. What is my deadline to enroll for benefits?

You become eligible for most health and welfare benefit plans on the first of the month following 30 days employment. You may enroll from your date of hire until your effective date. Please note, it may take 5-7 business days from your date of hire for your information to be available in the enrollment system. If that is the case, you will have until the end of the month you become effective to make your elections.
