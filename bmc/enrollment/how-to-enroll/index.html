How to Enroll


Before enrolling online or with a Benefits Counselor, you must download the Mobile Health App to verify your tobacco status.

After downloading the Mobile Health App, carefully review the information contained in your benefits guide before accessing the online enrollment system and making a selection.

Two Ways to Enroll


1. Online

Once you have clicked on “Enroll Here” button below, follow the prompts to register and create your personal login credentials. Please provide the following information in order to confirm your identity:

  • Last 4 of SSN

  • Associate ID

  • DOB (mm/dd/yyyy)


When you have entered the above information, click “Look Up” to confirm your identity. Once confirmed, continue to follow the prompts to create your account, login and enroll in your benefits.



2. Call and Speak with a Benefits Counselor.

You can call the Enrollment Call Center and speak with a Benefits Counselor who will answer your benefits questions and enroll you over the phone.

Call 888-894-7595
Monday - Friday, 9 am - 8 pm EST
