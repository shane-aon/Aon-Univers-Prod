Welcome to People’s United!

People’s United offers employees a competitive package of benefits that includes a choice of health plans, valuable life and disability insurance, retirement benefits and various wellness and work/life offerings.

As a new employee, you have 30 days following your first day of employment to enroll in the following benefits:

  • Medical/Prescription Drug
  • Voluntary Accidental Injury, Critical Illness and Hospital Care Insurances
  • Dental
  • Vision
  • Flexible Spending Accounts

Your benefits are important. Be sure you take this opportunity to choose the right benefits coverage for you and your family. If you miss your new hire enrollment deadline, you will not have People’s United health care coverage this year. You will not have another opportunity to enroll for People’s United health care benefits until either the next annual Open Enrollment period (for benefits effective January 1, 2020) or until you experience a permitted election change event, such as a marriage, birth or adoption.

WAYS TO ENROLL FOR BENEFITS

  • Online at www.my-peoples.com
    Online enrollment is available 24/7 on my-peoples during your new hire enrollment period, so you can enroll when it is convenient for you. Simply log into my-peoples and go to Myself>Life Events>I am a new hire. You must elect or decline every benefit listed, and then click “Submit.” The coverage you elect will take effect on the 1st of the month following your date of hire.

  • Over the phone by calling 877-625-6517, Monday - Friday, 9:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. ET
    Speak with a benefits counselor by calling the Enrollment Center during your new hire enrollment period. Your counselor will explain your benefits options, answer your questions, and help you make the best benefit decisions for you and your family.

If you have any questions, call the Enrollment Center at 877-625-6517or the HR Info Line at 877-274-8383.
