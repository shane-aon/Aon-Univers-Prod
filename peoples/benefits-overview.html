Benefits Overview

We have a comprehensive benefits package designed to promote employees’ health and wellness, financial wellbeing, professional development and career growth.

Health + Wellness


We care about your well-being and encourage you to take an active role in maintaining your health. We provide medical and dental plan options, vision benefits and access to wellness programs. Flexible Spending Accounts are available to pay for eligible health care and dependent care expenses on a pre-tax basis. Voluntary Critical illness, Accidental Injury and Hospital Care Insurances are also offered.
Life + Disability


We understand that protecting your income in case you’re unable to work is a priority. We offer company sponsored Life and Accidental Death & Dismemberment Insurance and Disability Insurance to help ensure you’re covered for the unexpected. You also have the option to elect Supplemental Group Universal Life and Personal Accident Insurance and Long Term Care Insurance.
Work/Life


Flexibility to take time off from work for vacation, personal obligations or illness is necessary. We offer competitive Paid Time Off and paid holidays to give you the opportunity to unwind, have fun and take care of yourself and family. We also offer Commuter Benefits with a subsidy for qualified transit options, a tuition assistance program, an Employee Assistance Program and more.
Retirement + Savings


Preparing for retirement is just as important as taking care of your needs today. Our 401(k) Savings Plan can help you save for retirement with pre-tax and Roth contributions, an Employer Match on your contributions and an Employer Retirement Contribution. We also offer a 100% employer-funded Employee Stock Ownership Plan.
