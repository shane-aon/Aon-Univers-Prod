WHAT’S NEW FOR 2019


Perdue is making the following changes to Your Perdue Benefits for 2019:


1. New Medical Plan Co-pays

We will continue to offer the Advantage 10 and Essential 20 health plan options for 2019, but now with in-network CO-PAYS!

  • $30 co-pay* – Primary Care Physician (PCP) Visits
  • $30 co-pay* – Urgent Care Centers
  • $50 co-pay* – Specialist Visits
  • $100 co-pay – Emergency Room (waived if admitted)

* Office visits will no longer be subject to the deductible and coinsurance.


2. New Medical Cards

New medical cards will be issued by CareFirst for ALL associates.*
* Medical cards to include co-pay.


3. New Prescription Drug Programs

Prescription drug programs being added are:

  • Opioid Management
  • Smart90 – CVS/Walgreens for maintenance medications with a deeper discount

4. Review your 2019 Associate Contribution Rates

See your rate sheet for details.
