HOW TO ENROLL

Click here

for instructions on setting up your online account and viewing your confirmation statement.

Review this website carefully. This information will help you to understand the benefit options available to you and how to elect coverage.

Your health care coverage is effective on the first of the month following your date of hire, rehire or status change to regular full-time. You must enroll for J.Crew benefits within 31 days of your coverage effective date.

Medical Underwriting (EOI) is required for coverage over the guaranteed amount or as a late entrant for Supplemental Life, Spouse Life and Supplemental LTD. Coverage is effective upon approval from the applicable carrier.

ENROLLMENT OPTIONS

There are two ways to elect coverage:

  1. Online: Visit https://uenroll.net/jcrew2018/ to access the online enrollment system. You will need the following information to log in:
    1. Username: Your SAP ID
    2. Password: “Jcrew” and last 4 digits of your Social Security Number (Jcrew####)

  2. Benefits Enrollment Call Center: Call the Benefits Enrollment Call Center to speak with a Benefits Counselor who will explain your benefits, answer your questions and process your enrollment over the phone.

    Call the Benefits Enrollment Call Center at
    1-888-827-8729
    Monday - Friday, 9:00 a.m. - 6:00 p.m. ET
