2019 Benefit Highlights

HBC makes the health and safety of our Associates a priority, and we strive to provide comprehensive, high quality healthcare benefits for you and your family at an affordable cost.

While there continues to be an unprecedented rise in healthcare costs across the nation, HBC is employing strategies to ensure you continue to have access to affordable, comprehensive and high-quality healthcare for you and your family.

For 2019, HBC is continuing our focus on your physical, mental and financial wellness. By helping you maintain and improve your overall health and well-being, we can work together to minimize both your and HBC’s overall healthcare costs in the coming years.

BELOW ARE BENEFIT HIGHLIGHTS FOR 2019.

CONSUMERMEDICAL (CM) IS YOUR MEDICAL ALLY!

ConsumerMedical (CM) is your Medical Ally for independent, confidential, one-on-one support to help you and your family make informed decisions about medical care and treatment at every point in your healthcare journey. CM’s services to participants in HBC’s medical plans include:


EXPERT MEDICAL GUIDANCE FOR ANY CONDITION, NO MATTER HOW COMPLEX OR RARE.

CM is your Medical Ally for all conditions and challenges. You will receive:

  • Support from your own dedicated team (including a doctor, nurse and professional researcher) over the phone (or via secure email or texting)
  • Recommendations for top-rated specialists and hospitals in your area and insurance networks who have experience with your medical condition
  • Guidance on how to talk to your doctor and ask the right questions
  • Help getting a second opinion when you need one
  • Articles, books, videos, and links to websites to help you better understand your medical condition and treatment options

EXPERT SURGICAL OPINIONS, REMOTE OR IN-PERSON.

CM provides direct access to in-person and remote medical expertise from the highest-quality providers for your unique condition, preferences and needs. CM partners with renowned medical institutions and leading physicians with extensive experience providing remote second opinions in the U.S. and globally. For In-Person Expert Medical Opinions, CM will match you with the most appropriate, highest performing physicians in your area for your specific medical condition, and will help you schedule your appointment.

For ALL Expert Opinions, CM will assist you with:

  • Medical records collection, pathology review and appointment scheduling
  • Unlimited concierge support from CM’s physician-led team
  • Questions for employees to ask their physicians
  • Education on the value of high-performing physicians

CLAIM ADVOCACY SERVICES

CM’s claims advocates are neutral experts who offer independent guidance to help you understand and resolve problems and reduce the stress of dealing with insurance and billing issues. CM’s Claims Advocacy service helps you:


  • Comprehend and interpret Explanation of Benefits (EOB)
  • Calculate out-of-pocket costs
  • Clarify coverage
  • Communicate with providers
  • Identify and fix billing errors
  • Manage health plan correspondence on claims issues
  • Navigate denials and appeals
  • Outreach to providers if needed

TARGETED SURGICAL DECISION SUPPORT

UNDERSTAND YOUR OPTIONS BEFORE HAVING SURGERY.

To help you understand your options before having surgery, there is a requirement under HBC’s health plans to participate in Surgery Decision Support (SDS) through ConsumerMedical for FIVE (5) specific surgeries:

  • Lower back surgery,
  • Hip or knee replacement,
  • Weight loss surgery, or
  • Hysterectomy

If your physician recommends one of these surgeries, you must contact ConsumerMedical for Surgery Decision Support (SDS) before having non-emergency.

If you, or your covered spouse or domestic partner, complete the SDS requirements, you will avoid $1,000 penalty at the time your claim(s) are processed, unless you have one of these surgeries on an emergency basis. To be eligible for gift card and avoid the penalty, contact ConsumerMedical at least 30 days before the scheduled date for one of the surgeries listed above to engage with ConsumerMedical through completion of a survey. You do not have to follow ConsumerMedical’s recommendations – the ultimate decision regarding your medical condition remains with you and your physician.

If you participate in the program for Oncology Support you will receive a $400 HSA contribution from HBC.


MAVEN MATERNITY PROGRAM

UNDERSTAND YOUR OPTIONS BEFORE HAVING SURGERY.

Maven Maternity is a comprehensive, digital, 15-month program that provides unlimited access to Maven’s virtual practitioner network, a dedicated Care Concierge, and customizable resources/ curriculum in one app. HBC medical plan participants are eligible to enroll in Maven Maternity at no cost. (HBC pays the full cost of the Maven program).

When you enroll in the Maven Maternity Program you will receive:

  • On-demand access to 1,200+ women’s and family health specialists via video chat and messaging
  • Pregnancy, postpartum, pediatric, and return-to-work support
  • Personal concierge to help you navigate your benefits
  • Helpful resources and a supportive parent community

In addition, effective January 1, 2019, HBC Associates who enroll in the Maven Maternity program in their first trimester of pregnancy will receive a $250 Lord & Taylor gift card upon program completion ($125 if enrolled during the second trimester).


OMADA

Omada Health is a digital behavioral medicine company that inspires and enables people to change the habits that put them most at risk for chronic conditions like heart disease and type 2 diabetes. The company is the largest CDC-recognized provider of the National Diabetes Prevention Program, and since its founding, has enrolled more than 120,000 participants. Eligible HBC medical plan members can enroll in the Omada program at no cost (the full cost of this program is paid by HBC).

COLOR

Color Genomics (Color) provides genetic testing services to help you understand your genetic risk for common hereditary cancers, inherited heart conditions and medication management. All U.S.-based full-time associates 18 years old or older who are HBC medical plan participants are eligible to access Color’s personalized genetic testing and counseling services at no cost (HBC pays the full cost of this program).

The genetic testing is done by a simple saliva test. Information on how to obtain your test kit will be sent to Associates in the next few weeks. You will receive your results in 3-4 weeks after mailing your test sample to Color. When you receive your results, you will then have an opportunity to discuss your results and suggested next steps with a Color genetic counselor and forward the results to your personal physician.

FREE MENTAL HEALTH ASSESSMENT

A first-time in-network mental health assessment will be covered with no deductible or coinsurance required.

PROGRAMS IN SUPPORT OF FAMILIES

INFERTILITY BENEFITS

Infertility Treatment, including Advanced Reproductive Therapy (IVF, Ziff, Giff, etc) is covered under our Gold and Silver CDHP medical plans, up to a lifetime maximum benefit of $10,000. The lifetime maximum benefit will be coordinated with Infertility prescription benefits under the CVS Prescription plan (combined medical and prescription maximum of $15,000) starting January 1, 2019.


SURROGACY ASSISTANCE

In addition to Adoption Assistance, HBC will provide reimbursement for qualifying Surrogacy Expenses up to $5,000 per surrogacy.

    PRESCRIPTION BENEFIT HIGHLIGHTS FOR 2019

    Your prescription plan is through CVS Health for 2019. Below are some important highlights:


    • Advanced Control Formulary: Certain brand drugs will be removed from the Formulary list from time to time when there are other lower-cost alternatives available. Affected Associates will be informed of alternatives directly by CVS.
    • CVS AccordantCare Rare program is available for 19 rare conditions, some of which include Multiple Sclerosis, Cycstic Fibrosis, Parkinson’s Disease, Sickle Cell Disease, Human Immunodeficiency Virus.

    CVS registered nurse care managers serve as liaisons and advocates, facilitating relationships between members, physicians, specialists, insurance companies and employers to ensure high-quality care delivery within realistic cost-containment strategies, and continually evaluate and coordinate treatment progress to make sure medical plan participants receive the most appropriate care for their individual needs.

    FLEXIBLE SPENDING ACCOUNTS (FSA)

    Remember you must enroll or re-enroll in a Health Care or Dependent Care FSA each year. Enrollment is not automatic and your current year elections do not carry over into the next year.

    Review this benefits website for more information on the benefits available to you, premiums, resources and enrollment instructions to help you with your decisions as a new hire, for Annual Enrollment and throughout the year.

    IMPORTANT INFORMATION ABOUT ONLINE ENROLLMENT

    When enrolling online you must complete the full enrollment process (including printing your confirmation statement) to ensure your elections are what you intended. Any changes you make in the Enrollment System are saved in “real time” meaning- as you make them. Therefore, if you leave the system after any enrollment section, any changes you have made up to that point will be saved. If you do not go back and complete the enrollment process through printing and reviewing your confirmation statement, your incomplete benefit elections will become your final benefit elections for 2019.
