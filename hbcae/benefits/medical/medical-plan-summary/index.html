Medical Plan Summary


Each person’s healthcare needs are different. That’s why our medical plan offers multiple options so that you can choose the coverage level best-suited to you and your family. You have three plans to choose from — Gold, Silver and Bronze. All three plans are administered by Cigna and include prescription drug coverage at no additional cost.

Click on the plan column heading below to view each plan's Summary of Benefits. For more details on coverage, view the additional plan documents on the Documents/Forms page. You can also use the Quick Comparison Tool to help in your selection process.

Infertility Benefits

Infertility Treatment, including Advanced Reproductive Therapy (IVF, Ziff, Giff, etc) will now be covered under our Gold and Silver CDHP medical plans, up to a lifetime maximum benefit of $10,000. The lifetime maximum benefit will be coordinated with Infertility prescription benefits under the CVS Prescription plan (combined medical and prescription maximum of $15,000) starting January 1, 2019.


INDIVIDUAL MAXIMUMS:

The plan deductibles and out-of-pocket maximums are based on the coverage tier you elected. If you are enrolled in Associate Only coverage, you would be responsible to meet the “single” deductible and out-of-pocket maximum. If you are enrolled in Associate + Spouse/Domestic Partner, Associate + Children or Associate + Family coverage, the “family” deductible and out-of-pocket maximum apply. In addition, there are individual maximums for the Silver and Bronze plans. This means if you elect family coverage (Associate + Spouse/Domestic Partner, Associate + Children or Associate + Family), the most an individual family member would be responsible to pay out-of-pocket for the year when using in-network providers would be $6,550.


A NOTE ON PREMIUMS:

Spousal Surcharge: If your spouse has access to coverage through his/her employer, and you choose to cover him/her on an HBC plan, you will incur a $25 surcharge on your weekly medical premium.

Non-Tobacco Use Discount: If you certify that you have not used tobacco and/or nicotine delivery products for 12 months or more, you will be eligible to receive a discount on your medical plan premium.
