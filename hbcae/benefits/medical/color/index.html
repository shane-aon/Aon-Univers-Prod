Color


Discover what your genes say about your health with Color Genomics (Color) genetic health services. Color helps you understand your genetic risk for common hereditary cancers, inherited heart conditions and medication management. All U.S.-based full-time associates 18 years old or older who are HBC medical plan participants are eligible to access Color’s personalized genetic testing and counseling services at no cost (HBC pays the full cost of this program). Learn more in this educational webinar and request your complimentary Color kit here.

Color’s service includes the following results:


  • Hereditary Cancer
    Analyzes 30 genes, including BRCA1 and BRCA2, in which a mutation can increase one’s likelihood of developing hereditary breast, ovarian, uterine, colorectal, melanoma, pancreatic, stomach, or prostate cancers.

  • Heart Health
    Analyzes 30 genes commonly associated with hereditary heart conditions that can have actionable treatment plans, including heart muscle disease, irregular heartbeat, artery disease, and hereditary high cholesterol.

  • Medication Management Results
    Helps you and your provider understand which medications are best for you based on your genes to minimize harmful side effects and get more effective care.

  • Color Discovery Results
    Get insights from your DNA on an ongoing basis to help you learn more about common traits that are influenced by your genes, such as lactose intolerance and alcohol flush.

The genetic testing is done by a simple saliva test. Information on how to obtain your test kit will be sent to Associates in the next few weeks. You will receive your results in 3-4 weeks after mailing your test sample to Color. When you receive your results, you will then have an opportunity to discuss your results and suggested next steps with a Color genetic counselor and forward the results to your personal physician.

Color services are completely confidential. Color takes privacy very seriously will NOT provide any personally identifiable health information to HBC of Associates who use Color.
