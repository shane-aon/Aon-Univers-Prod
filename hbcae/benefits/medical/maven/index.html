Maven Maternity


HBC has partnered with Maven to offer on-demand maternity, postpartum, and return-to-work support to Associates who are enrolled in one of HBC’s medical benefit plans as well as their enrolled partners. Maven is a maternity and family health benefit that is fully covered by HBC.

Sign up for instant access to the largest virtual practitioner network dedicated to women’s and family health. Video chat or private message with OB-GYNs, lactation consultants, infant sleep experts, career coaches, and more—anytime you need to, from wherever you are.

The Maven Maternity Program is available to parents during pregnancy and up to 6 months postpartum. Upon sign up, you’ll get a dedicated concierge who will stay with you throughout the program to help you navigate your HBC benefits, provide in-person referrals, and assist you in accessing Maven’s curated practitioner network.

Upon completion of your participation in the Maven Maternity Program you will receive a Lord & Taylor gift card for $250 (if you enroll in your first trimester) or $125 (if you enroll in your second trimester).

Visit http://www.mavenclinic.com/join/hbc2019 to register and learn more.
