About the Administrator

Founded as Future Planning Associates in 1985, Aon Voluntary Benefits & Enrollment Solutions, who purchased the Company in 2004, has been serving the insurance and financial needs of its policyholders for more than two decades. Presently, Aon Voluntary Benefits & Enrollment Solutions services employees in a wide spectrum of job categories, including 2,000,000 educators, members of labor unions, and professional associations.

While NYSUT members always have direct access to Transamerica Financial Life Insurance Company, Aon Voluntary Benefits & Enrollment Solutions provides assistance by rendering personalized, expedient service to improve the flow of information and requests for service.

About the Insurer

Transamerica Financial Life Insurance Company (TFLIC), founded in 1947, is apart of the worldwide group of Aegon companies. As an international life insurance, pensions and asset management company based in The Hague, Aegon has businesses in over twenty markets in the Americas, Europe and Asia. TFLIC is rated A+ by A.M. Best. The Home Office is located in Harrison, New York.
