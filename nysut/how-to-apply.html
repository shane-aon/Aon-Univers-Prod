Applying for WrapPlan®II

If you are interested in applying for WrapPlan®II, or, if you would like additional information, please contact the Plan Administrator, Aon Voluntary Benefits & Enrollment Solutions, toll-free at 866-NYSUT-WP (866-697-8897) to speak with a licensed Benefit Representative. Representatives are available Monday through Friday from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm (est).

You may apply for WrapPlan®II via telephone, or you may schedule an appointment to meet with a licensed representative. For your convenience, representatives are available evenings and weekends in addition to regular business hours.

Payment Options

There are several convenient options available to NYSUT members for the remittance of premium payments:

  • Payroll Deduction (where available) : based on your pay cycle
  • Pension Deduction: monthly deduction from your check
  • Checking Withdrawal: monthly deductions from your checking account
  • Credit Card: initial payment and/or on a recurring basis
  • Direct Billing: available on a quarterly, semi-annual or annual basis

Choose the payment option which is most convenient for you.
