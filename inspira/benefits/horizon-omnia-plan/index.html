Horizon Omnia Plan

The Horizon Omnia Plan gives you access to three network tiers of providers, allowing you to take greater control of how you spend your healthcare dollars:

  1. Inspira Health Inner Circle:
    Inspira Health providers and facilities; reduced or zero copays. When you receive care, you pay the least at Inner Circle providers and facilities.

  2. Horizon Omnia Tier 1:
    Horizon affiliated NJ providers and facilities determined by Horizon to be high-quality and low-cost.

  3. Horizon Omnia Tier 2:
    Additional Horizon providers and facilities, both in NJ and across the USA.

Aside from Emergency care, out-of-network services are not covered under the Horizon Omnia Plan.

The best feature of the Omnia Plan is how much you can save on out-of-pocket expenses. By accessing the Inspira Inner Circle tier of physicians and facilities, you will often pay nothing for most medical services.

Primary and Preventative Care visits in the Inspira Health Inner Circle cost $0. All hospital services and laboratory tests also come at no out-of-pocket cost.

There is no deductible to meet for the Inspira Health Inner Circle Tier.

Copays and deductibles will apply if you access care with either the Omnia Tier 1 or Omnia Tier 2 providers and facilities.

Inspira Health strives to offer you complete choice in health care.

You have two plan options to choose from with Horizon: the Horizon MyWay HSA Plan and the Horizon Omnia Plan. More detailed information outlining differences between the plans can be found in the 2019-2020 Benefits Guide and Horizon BCBS NJ Guide.

What's The Better Option For Me?

I currently access a provider that is out-of-network with Horizon and not participating in Omnia Tier 1 or Tier 2. Additionally, I would like to have the flexibility to save for future medical needs by putting money in a Health Savings Account.

A. Horizon MyWay HSA may be the better choice.

I’ll likely make quite a few trips to medical providers in the coming year and may need to use hospital services. I mostly use Inspira Health Inner Circle providers and facilities. My current providers are participating in one of the three Omnia plan network tiers.

A. Horizon Omnia may be the better choice.
