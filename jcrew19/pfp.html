*SPOUSE/DOMESTIC PARTNER SURCHARGE

If your spouse/domestic partner is eligible for medical coverage through his/her employer and you elect to cover him/her under a J.Crew medical plan, you will incur a $100 monthly surcharge. An associate who intentionally falsifies his/her spouse/domestic partner as not having access to coverage through an employer will be immediately subject to the spouse/domestic partner surcharge and may be subjected to termination of the health plan with responsibility for all claims incurred, as well as discipline up to and including termination of employment.

1 Due to IRS regulations, you must pay taxes on the value of the medical, dental and vision benefit you receive for your domestic partner and any children of your domestic partner that you cover.  The payroll deductions throughout the year will be deducted on a pre-tax basis, but will be included in the imputed income calculation at year end to be reflected in your W-2.
