The Galaxy Savings Plan

  • You are eligible to participate in the Galaxy Savings Plan if you are a Non-Union associate or in a job class covered by a collective bargaining agreement that provides you to participate in the Plan. If you are uncertain whether you are eligible to participate in this Plan, contact Wells Fargo at 1-700-728-31233
  • The Retirement Savings (401k) benefit is not able to be updated through this Online Enrollment process. Please contact Wells Fargo to enroll or make changes.
  • Contact Wells Fargo at 1-700-728-31233 or www.wellsfargo.com/retirement to enroll, review and make any necessary changes to The Galaxy Savings Plan. It's that easy to save for the future, Enroll in the plan today.

The Galaxy Retirement Account Plan
(Pension Plan)

  • You may also be eligible for a benefit from the Galaxy Farms Retirement Account Plan if you were hired before October 2, 2001. If you are eligible for this Plan you should also have a designated beneficiary on file.
  • Contact your Human Resources office for any additional information concerning eligibility and the beneficiary form to make any necessary changes.
