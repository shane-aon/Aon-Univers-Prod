Enrollment Instruction


You must complete the enrollment process, either online, with an onsite enrollment counselor or by phone. By the end of your enrollment period, if you do not enroll, you will lose coverage for 2019.

Enrollment Options


1. Online

You may log onto the online enrollment system at any time during your enrollment period to record your benefit elections.


2. By Phone

You may call 555-555-555 (Monday - Friday, 9 a.m. - 6 p.m. EST to enroll with a Aon Enrollment Counselor. Calling earlier in your enrollment window, rather than waiting until the last minute, reduces the chances of being placed on hold. Translators are available by request for Associates who speak a language other than English.


What You Will Need

  • Your name, Associate ID Number and your date of birth.

  • Your mailing address and telephone number

  • If a new dependent is being added, documentation may be required.

You MUST Participate in an Enrollment Session

If you do not participate, you will be auto-enrolled into certain coverages, and your family members will not be covered.

Coverage If you do not participate in an enrollment session...
Health Plan You will lose coverage.
Spouse and/or Child Coverage Family members will NOT be covered unless you elect coverage for them during your enrollment period.
Long Term Disability You will lose coverage.
Flexible Spending Account (Health Care or Dependent Care) or Health Savings Account No contributions will be made.
Voluntary Benefit Options You will miss the opportunity to enroll in these plans without having to provide evidence of good health for certain elections.
