2019 Medical Plan Options/Summaries


The Your Galaxy Benefits Medical Plans offer easy access to qualified medical providers through the Wellness Centers or at the office of a doctor. You can elect one of two Galaxy Health Plan options. Both plans offer care under the national CareFirst BCBS BluePreferred PPO Plan. The plans include prescription drug coverage through Express Scripts at no additional cost..
